5e Conversion Notes

Weapons: RED's damage dice assume ROF-based combat with no Extra Attack. In 5e, a Fighter with Extra Attack swings 2–4 times per round. All weapon damage has been scaled down to standard 5e ranges (1d4–2d6 for melee, 1d6–2d10 for ranged). Properties use 5e keywords: Light, Finesse, Heavy, Two-Handed, Loading, Ammunition, etc.

Armor: RED's SP/ablation system is gone. All armor uses standard 5e AC (Light = base + DEX, Medium = base + DEX max 2, Heavy = flat AC). No SP, no ablation, no REF/DEX/MOVE penalties beyond what 5e already handles with Stealth disadvantage and STR requirements.

Cyberware: RED skill bonuses (e.g., "+2 to Perception") become 5e equivalents (+2 to Wisdom (Perception) checks, or advantage on specific checks). Popup weapons reference 5e weapon stat lines. Subdermal armor becomes AC bonuses.

Concealable: A concealable weapon can be hidden on your person. Detecting it requires a Wisdom (Perception) check contested by the carrier's Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check.

Burst Fire (New Property)

A weapon with the Burst Fire property can be fired in short controlled bursts. As an Action, you can spray a 10-foot cube within normal range. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 15 DEX save or take the weapon's normal damage. This uses 10 rounds of ammunition.

Loading (Adapted)

A weapon with the Loading property fires a single shot per Attack action, regardless of Extra Attack or other features that grant additional attacks. You can still use a Bonus Action or other class features on the same turn — you just can't fire the weapon more than once. This applies to sniper rifles, rocket launchers, and grenade launchers.

Specialty Ammo

Specialty rounds cost 5× the standard ammo price and change the weapon's damage type or add an effect. Sold in the same quantities as standard ammo.

TypeEffect
Armor-PiercingDamage type becomes Force. Bypasses resistances. Damage dice reduced by one step (3d10 → 3d8, 2d8 → 2d6, etc).
IncendiaryDamage type becomes Fire. On a critical hit, the target catches fire (1d4 Fire damage at the start of each of their turns until they use an Action to extinguish).
EMPDamage type becomes Lightning. On hit, one piece of the target's cyberware is disabled for 1 round (DM's choice). Damage dice reduced by one step.
Rubber / Non-LethalDamage type becomes Bludgeoning. All damage is non-lethal (target falls unconscious at 0 HP instead of making death saves).
